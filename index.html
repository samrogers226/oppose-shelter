Neighbors Against Low Threshold Shelters

Since the "temporary" opening of the low threshold shelter at the former Envision Hotel in November 2021, our neighborhood has declined significantly. Theft, vandalism, loitering, littering, open drug use, and discarded needles are now a part of daily life. The operator of the shelter is attempting to change the zoning to make this shelter a permanent fixture in our neighborhood. We cannot let this stand.
